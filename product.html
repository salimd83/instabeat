Instabeat is the first modular biometric tool that allows you to monitor your heart rate while swimming. It can be mounted onto the straps of favorite pair of swimming goggles, and integrates all of the electronics, getting rid of the need for any external chest belt, ear or finger clip.

We will keep all of your information confidential and we will never send you any junk. We will only send you an email once the product is ready to ship.
